I am a third year PhD student in the Computer Science Department (CICS) at University of Massachusetts Amherst. I am fortunate to be advised by Arya Mazumdar. Prior to this I graduated from Indian Institute of Technology, Kharagpur in August 2016 with a Bachelors degree in Electronics and Electrical Communication Engineering.

Research

''What is unseen, is not necessarily unknown'' - Wendelessen

My research interests are statistical machine learning, information theory and coding theory. I have worked on problems related to random graph theory, high dimensional integration and semi-supervised clustering. Recently, I am interested in the interesting traces reconstruction problem and other problems corresponding to the random deletion channel.
